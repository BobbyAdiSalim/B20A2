As a user, the student must be able to see the home page of the course website when clicking on the website link.

Student must be able to see the course description, required background, motivation, course learning outcomes, and instructor information.

User can also directly email the instructor by clicking the e-mail section. 

On the left of the website, there is a navigation bar where user can access different pages by clicking on the navigation bar.

On the bottom, there is also a navigation bar and links to the CMS socials.

On the top, there is a "Back to Top" navigation button to let user immediately and automatically scroll back up after scrolling down the page.
